About Me

I'm a 19-year-old university student who got tired of watching brilliant software get locked behind expensive subscriptions. Why should creativity cost a monthly fee?

My belief is simple: with AI tools and the right skills, indie developers can build equivalents of paid apps — and open source them for everyone. That's exactly what I'm doing, one app at a time.

Every project under Forgotten Disciple is and will always remain free, open source, and GPL v3 licensed. The code belongs to everyone. No exceptions.
